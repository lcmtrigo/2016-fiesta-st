Meet the high-performance Fiesta ST

With a 1.6L EcoBoost® engine that delivers 197 horsepower, 17-inch Rado Gray-painted aluminum wheels and ST-unique sport suspension, this is definitely a driver's car. As a proud member of the Ford Performance Family, it boasts aluminum pedals, available race-inspired RECARO® leather-trimmed front seats and special ST design elements.

*Tested with 93-octane fuel.

Fiesta ST Steering Features

  • Modified steering knuckle is designed to help increase wheel camber and quicken steering
  • Electric power-assisted steering (EPAS) delivers light, responsive steering at low speeds, less steering assist at higher speeds for feeling of control
  • EPAS includes active nibble control, which uses the EPAS motor to reduce unwanted oscillations in the steering wheel
